Cinte International West Point College has a number of professional entrepreneurship support teachers. Six major entrepreneurship support policies are presented free of charge. They regularly guide students to start their own businesses, operate and diagnose them, so that students can make profits and start from their entrepreneurial orientation. Dedicated to Western food entrepreneurship training success rate.

辛缇国际西餐学院

课程现场

辛缇国际西餐学院，采用小班教学模式，单班次人数不超过12人，老师一对一进行教学，所有课程99%的实操，五年会免费复训，不限学习时间，学会为止。香港设立独立研发中心，每月更新新品，所有学员同步更新。
Cinte International Western Food College, using a small class teaching model, the single class number of no more than 12 people, teachers one to one teaching, all courses 99% practice, five years of free training, unlimited study time, Hong Kong set up an independent R & D center, monthly update new products, all students update synchronously.

Cinte teacher team includes technical training team and entrepreneurship support team, all teachers are senior titles.

辛缇国际介绍

辛缇国际西餐学院为辛缇国际（香港）集团旗下教育品牌，专注于西餐创业实战培训，全国连锁，校区辐射五大城市CINTE INTERNATIONAL WESTERN FOOD INSTITUTE IS AN EDUCATIONAL BRAND OWNED BY SINTI INTERNATIONAL (HONG KONG) GROUP, FOCUSING ON THE FIVE MAJOR CITIES OF WESTERN FOOD ENTREPRENEURSHIP TRAINING, NATIONAL CHAIN AND CAMPUS RADIATION.
